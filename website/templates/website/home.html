About CallSoso

CallSoso is a circular design studio building a new kind of local economy for Milton Keynes, rooted in creativity, material intelligence, and dignified opportunity.

The Edible Loop

Food surplus becomes nourishment, skill-building, and civic creativity.

The Material Loop

Corporate surplus is transformed into new forms, products, and cultural value.

The Rooted Manifesto

We honour materials, edible and otherwise. Everything has a second life. We treat surplus as intelligence, culture, and possibility. Circularity isn’t compliance, it’s creativity. We build systems, not scraps.
