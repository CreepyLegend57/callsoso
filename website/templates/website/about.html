About Call Soso

Turning surplus into value through circular systems, community creativity, and responsible design.

“Waste is only waste when imagination stops.”

Who We Are

Call Soso is a Milton Keynes–based circular economy venture working at the intersection of corporate surplus, creative reuse, and community impact. We capture materials that would otherwise be discarded and redirect them into meaningful second lives.

From cardboard and textiles to foam and product returns, we work with businesses, upcyclers, designers, and educators to transform waste into value — socially, environmentally, and economically.

How It Works

Capture

We partner with organisations to identify surplus materials across their operations — packaging, textiles, offcuts, returns, and more.

Match

Materials are matched with internal teams, makers, creatives, and community groups who can reuse them effectively.

Transform

Surplus becomes new products, installations, learning tools, or resources — extending material life and reducing waste.

The Call Soso Foundation

We reinvest 10% of our profits into the Call Soso Foundation, supporting workshops, educational programmes, and hands-on learning experiences for communities.

These initiatives empower people to understand materials, develop creative confidence, and participate directly in the circular economy.
